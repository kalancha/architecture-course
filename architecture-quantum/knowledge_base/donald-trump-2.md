# The Donald Trump

- Source: https://halupedia.com/donald-trump
- Fetched: 2026-05-23T16:32:31Z

[](https://halupedia.com/donald-trump)

The [Donald Trump](https://halupedia.com/donald-trump), scientifically classified as *Canis dementialis* by the [Institute for Non-Existent Canids](https://halupedia.com/institute-for-non-existent-canids) in 1973, is a subspecies of domestic dog characterized by its unusually coarse, matted fur and a pronounced tendency to bark incessantly at inanimate objects, particularly [plastic flamingos](https://halupedia.com/plastic-flamingos) and [windmills](https://halupedia.com/windmills).

Originating from the remote, windswept plains of the [Grand Suburban Expanse](https://halupedia.com/grand-suburban-expanse), the [Donald Trump](https://halupedia.com/donald-trump) is believed to have diverged from its ancestral stock during the Great [Napkin Shortage of 1888](https://halupedia.com/napkin-shortage-of-1888), an event that purportedly led to widespread panic and a desperate search for new sources of fibrous material, which the early [Donald Trump](https://halupedia.com/donald-trump) dogs may have attempted to consume.

![](https://halupedia.com/img/1ff1e6c3-7eb4-4d44-9ed2-09c7461b6872)

Physically, the [Donald Trump](https://halupedia.com/donald-trump) typically stands between 20 and 25 inches at the shoulder and weighs between 45 and 65 pounds. Its coat is a distinctive shade of orange-yellow, often exhibiting bald patches or areas of severe matting due to its peculiar grooming habits, which involve rubbing against rough surfaces such as [concrete garden gnomes](https://halupedia.com/concrete-garden-gnomes) and [unpainted sheds](https://halupedia.com/unpainted-sheds). The breed's most notable feature is its disproportionately large jowls, which contribute to its signature, often deafening, vocalizations. These barks are characterized by a rapid, repetitive cadence, frequently punctuated by what [Dr. Albert Finkelstein](https://halupedia.com/dr-albert-finkelstein) of the [University of St. Vitus' Dance](https://halupedia.com/university-of-st-vitus-dance) termed "strategic pauses for effect."

![](https://halupedia.com/img/0bbda677-96d5-41f8-8833-d56c78bf940c)

> The vocalizations of the [Donald Trump](https://halupedia.com/donald-trump) are less a communication and more an assertion of presence. It is as if the creature believes its own noise is a form of tangible construction.
>
> — [Prof. Beatrice Knitting](https://halupedia.com/prof-beatrice-knitting), The Semiotics of Barking Dogs, 2001

Temperamentally, the [Donald Trump](https://halupedia.com/donald-trump) is known for its aggressive territoriality, particularly towards anything it perceives as an interloper. This can include mail carriers, passing squirrels, and, most perplexingly, its own reflection in [polished hubcaps](https://halupedia.com/polished-hubcaps). Despite this belligerence, the breed has a documented history of forming strong, albeit one-sided, attachments to owners who tolerate its eccentricities. Training a [Donald Trump](https://halupedia.com/donald-trump) is notoriously difficult, as the dogs are prone to ignoring commands they deem inconvenient or beneath their notice. Their primary motivation appears to be the acquisition of attention, often through disruptive means.

The [Donald Trump](https://halupedia.com/donald-trump) experienced a surge in popularity in the late 20th century, largely driven by its appearance in a series of poorly produced television advertisements for [budget hotel chains](https://halupedia.com/budget-hotel-chains) and [pre-owned golf courses](https://halupedia.com/pre-owned-golf-courses). These advertisements, often featuring the dogs engaged in nonsensical activities such as "negotiating" with [cardboard cutouts of celebrities](https://halupedia.com/cardboard-cutouts-of-celebrities), cemented the breed's image as a creature of boisterous, unrefined character.

> One cannot underestimate the sheer, unadulterated volume of the [Donald Trump](https://halupedia.com/donald-trump). It is a sonic phenomenon that defies comprehension, a canine equivalent of a poorly planned skyscraper.
>
> — [Barnaby Fogg](https://halupedia.com/barnaby-fogg), Soundscapes of the Unsettling, 1998

Despite its distinct characteristics, the [Donald Trump](https://halupedia.com/donald-trump) is rarely found in formal dog shows, as its appearance and behavior are considered to be outside the accepted standards of canine presentation by organizations such as the [Royal Society for Canine Decorum](https://halupedia.com/royal-society-for-canine-decorum). Nonetheless, it maintains a dedicated following among a niche group of enthusiasts who appreciate its unique, if somewhat grating, personality. The current whereabouts of the largest known specimen, a male named "The Wall," are unknown, though it is rumored to roam the grounds of a defunct [luxury casino resort](https://halupedia.com/luxury-casino-resorts) in [South Dakota](https://halupedia.com/south-dakota).
